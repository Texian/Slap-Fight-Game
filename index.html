It's a slapdown!

The goal is simple: slap-out your opponent before he slaps you out!

Flip a coin to see who slaps first. Then, decide how you're going to attack or block. Beware! The loser gets two attacks in a row!

If the attack and block are the same height, the block succeeds. Otherwise, someone's getting slapped into next Tuesday.

Be the last slapper standing!
